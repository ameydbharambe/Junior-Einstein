# ChatBot-Project
From my personal experience, often when you are first stepping into research and reading papers, you often feel that there is a gap between the knowledge of the author of the paper and the reader who often has just entered this domain. I wanted to bridge this gap by using AI to simplify research papers into basic language. This includes using analogies that anyone can understand such as cooking and its relevance to the real world. To do so, I experimented with the Gemini API and pdf reading libraries. The motivation to choose the Gemini API over GPT and Claude comes from a more cost effective perspective.
